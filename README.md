# DayTone 🌈

A minimalist daily mood-tracking app built with **Expo Router** and **React Native**. Each day you pick a color that matches your mood, creating a soothing GitHub-style heatmap of your emotional year.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🎨 **Color-based mood tracking** — Tap any day and choose a tone that reflects your mood.
- 🗓️ **Interactive heatmap** — Each square represents a day; colors fill in as you log moods.
- 💾 **Offline storage** — Moods are saved locally using AsyncStorage.
- 📆 **Year view** — Displays moods for the past 365 days.
- 📝 **Optional notes** — Add small thoughts or highlights for any day.
- ⚡ **Expo Router support** — Organized, scalable, and ready for new screens.

---

## 🖌️ Design Philosophy

DayTone is about simplicity — quick taps, soft colors, and a sense of calm. It’s not meant to be another productivity app, but a space for gentle reflection. Every color is a reminder that your emotions form a pattern worth noticing.
